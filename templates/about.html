About Heart Disease Predictor

Welcome to our Heart Disease Prediction Tool!

This platform leverages advanced machine learning to analyze your health data and provide an early risk assessment for heart disease. Our goal is to empower individuals with insights that encourage preventive care and healthier lifestyles.

How It Works

Using data-driven insights, this tool assesses risk levels based on key health parameters such as blood pressure, cholesterol, and age, offering personalized predictions tailored to your health information. By identifying risks early, it aims to help you make informed decisions about your health.

Our Vision

To support proactive healthcare by providing accessible, reliable, and accurate heart disease predictions, helping people take control of their well-being.

Key Features

  • Advanced machine learning for precise predictions
  • Data-driven insights based on key health parameters
  • Easy-to-use interface for quick risk assessments
